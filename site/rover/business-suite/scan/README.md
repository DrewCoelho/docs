# Rover Scan

<PageHeader />

![Rover Scan Logo](/assets/img/rover-scan.png)

Rover Scan is the modern, mobile friendly web-based warehouse module of Rover Hub. Replace your legacy, telnet based handheld devices in favor of modern tablets and scanners to super charge your warehouse workforce. Available to users of many different ERPs, including but not limited to Rover, Smart Suite, Foresite, Crescendo, IMACS, Proman, SHIMS, AutoSHIMS, MIS, CUBS and more.
